Pasta

Description

This pasta is called "Cacio e Pepe", meaning "cheese and pepper"

It is the perfect dinner for when you cannot be bothered to cook as it consists of pantry ingredients and comes together in under 20 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Black pepper
  • Chilli flakes
  • Salt
  • Cheese
  • Spaghetti
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of water to a boil and place the spaghetti inside, along with a sprinkle of salt
  2. Chop the garlic
  3. Oil a pan and place the garlic inside
  4. Grate some cheese into the pan
  5. Stir to ensure that the cheese and oil become incorporated
  6. etc etc...
  7. Serve
